Twitter Bot

Creating the Twitter bot was easily the hardest part of the course. While it was taking me countless classes to finish, it took others 1 period. I reflected on what was taking me so long, and I realized that the reason for my struggles was that I wassn't watching Dan Shiffman's videos in their entirety. After realizing this, I began to watch them with audio, and to their conclusions and I was able to finish the bot in 1 hour.

Where do I go from here?

Throughout the course, I realized that I enjoy coding, and because of this passion, I was able to achieve my goals for the course. I stuck with the Twitter bot, and in the end, I succeeded. I believe that this passion will guide me to a career in Computer Science in the future.
