Story

You are an alien dubbed 'Subject 94', and you're being held hostage by human colonists who want to experiment on you. An alien of your species rescues you, but they want to exact revenge on the humans so you split. Your alien friend gets captured and experimented upon which leads to him getting stronger. Throughout your journey, you encouter other aliens of your species who give you unique powers. At the end of your journey, you re-encounter your friend, and his fate is in your hands.
